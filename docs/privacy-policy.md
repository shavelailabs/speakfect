# Speakfect Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 24, 2026**

Owned and operated by ShaVel AI Labs LLC, a California limited liability company ("we", "us", or "our").

---

## 1. What This Policy Covers

This Privacy Policy explains how Speakfect collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Speakfect mobile app and related services ("Service").

---

## 2. Definitions

- **Service:** The Speakfect mobile app and related features.
- **Practice Content:** Audio, video, transcripts, scripts, and other content you create or upload during practice sessions.
- **Personal Information:** Any information that identifies you, such as your name, email address, or device identifier.
- **Third-Party Providers:** External companies that help us deliver the Service (authentication, AI processing, hosting, crash reporting).

---

## 3. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account info:** Name, email, unique ID from authentication provider (Clerk, Google, Apple).
- **Profile info:** Optional details (profession, age range, country, language, communication goals).
- **Practice Content:** Audio/video/transcripts, scripts, Q&A, ratings, feedback.
- **Support messages:** Feedback sent via app or email.

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage data:** Features used, screens viewed, session counts, timestamps.
- **Device data:** Model, OS version, app version, language, time zone, device ID.
- **Network data:** API requests, IP address (used only for rate limits and abuse prevention; not linked to your profile or shared).
- **Crash/diagnostic data:** Error logs via Sentry.

### Information from Third Parties

- **Authentication providers:** Unique ID and basic profile fields.

We do not collect precise location, contacts, photos (except recordings you choose to save), or microphone/camera content outside practice sessions.

---

## 4. How We Use Information

- **Provide the Service:** Record sessions, generate AI feedback, track progress.
- **Improve the Service:** Analyze aggregate usage, fix bugs, prioritize features.
- **Communicate:** Respond to support requests, send notifications.
- **Protect:** Detect abuse, prevent fraud, enforce Terms of Service.
- **Legal:** Comply with laws.

We do not use your Practice Content to train any AI models, ours or third parties'.

---

## 5. AI Processing

To generate coaching feedback, your Practice Content (transcripts and, where supported, audio metadata) are sent to Google's Gemini API. Google processes this content under its own [Gemini API Terms](https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/terms). When accessed via the paid API, Google does not use your content to train its models.

We do not send raw video recordings to Gemini. Body-language and eye-contact metrics are computed on your device.

---

## 6. Sharing Information

We share information only in these cases:

- **Service providers** (Clerk, Google Gemini API, Sentry, Replit) — bound by contracts to protect your data.
- **Legal requirements** — when required by law or to protect rights/safety.
- **Business transfers** — if Speakfect is acquired or merged, you'll be notified before your info is subject to a different policy.

We do not sell your personal information.

---

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

- Access, correct, or delete your data.
- Export your data.
- Object to or restrict processing.
- Withdraw consent.

Delete your account anytime from **Profile → Delete account**. Deletion is permanent and immediate, except where legally required to retain records (e.g., billing, fraud prevention). You can set automatic deletion of practice transcripts in **Profile → Preferences**.

To exercise your rights, contact us at **privacy@speakfect.ai**. We respond within 30 days.

### EU/UK Residents (GDPR)

- Legal bases: contract, consent, legitimate interests.
- Right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

### California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

- Right to know, delete, and opt out of sale/sharing (we do not sell/share for advertising).

---

## 8. Data Retention

We keep your account and Practice Content while your account is active. When you delete your account, all personal data is removed within 30 days, except where legally required.

You can set automatic deletion of practice transcripts in **Profile → Preferences**.

---

## 9. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards (encryption in transit and at rest, access controls). No method is 100% secure.

If we become aware of a security breach affecting your data, we'll notify you and authorities as required, as soon as reasonably possible.

---

## 10. Children's Privacy

Speakfect is not for children under 13 (or the equivalent age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect information from children under that age. If you believe we have, contact **privacy@speakfect.ai** and we will promptly delete it.

---

## 11. International Data Transfers

Speakfect operates from the United States. If you use the Service from outside the US, your information will be transferred to and processed in the US. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses and other safeguards for EU/UK transfers.

---

## 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. When we make material changes, we'll notify you in the app and update the "Last updated" date. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
